## Getting Started

### Accessing XTracker

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the XTracker application URL
2. You'll see the login page if you already have an account, or you can create a new account

---

## Creating an Account

### Sign Up

1. Click on the **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"** button
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Name**: Your full name
   - **Email**: A valid email address (this will be your login username)
   - **Password**: Choose a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Click **"Sign Up"** to create your account
4. You'll be automatically logged in after successful registration

### Logging In

1. Enter your **email address** and **password**
2. Click **"Login"**
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

---

## Adding Expenses

### Quick Add

1. Navigate to the **"Expenses"** page from the sidebar
2. Click the **"Add Expense"** button
3. Fill in the expense form:
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown (Food & Dining, Transportation, Shopping, etc.)
   - **Description**: Brief description of the expense
   - **Date**: Select the date when the expense occurred
   - **Vendor**: Name of the vendor or merchant
   - **Amount**: Enter the expense amount (numbers only)
4. Click **"Save"** to add the expense

### Tips for Adding Expenses

- Be consistent with vendor names (e.g., always use "Walmart" instead of mixing "Walmart" and "Walmart Store")
- Use descriptive descriptions to help with future searches
- Add expenses regularly to maintain accurate records

## Viewing Expenses

### Expense List

The **Expenses** page displays all your expenses in a table format with:
- Category
- Description
- Date
- Vendor
- Amount

### Sorting and Filtering

- Expenses are sorted by date (newest first) by default
- Use the search/filter options to find specific expenses
- Filter by category, date range, or vendor

---

## Managing Expenses

### Editing an Expense

1. Find the expense you want to edit in the expense list
2. Click the **"Edit"** button (pencil icon)
3. Modify the fields you want to change
4. Click **"Save"** to update

### Deleting an Expense

1. Find the expense you want to delete
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion

**Note**: Deleted expenses cannot be recovered. Make sure you want to delete before confirming.

## Reports and Analytics

### Dashboard Overview

The **Dashboard** provides a quick overview of your spending:

- **Total Expenses**: Sum of all expenses
- **This Month**: Total expenses for the current month
- **Average per Expense**: Average amount per expense
- **Total Expenses Count**: Number of expenses recorded

### Reports Page

Navigate to the **Reports** page to access detailed analytics:

#### Category Report
- View spending breakdown by category
- See total amount and percentage for each category
- Visual charts showing category distribution

#### Vendor Report
- See which vendors you spend the most with
- Track spending patterns by vendor
- Identify top vendors by total amount

#### Period Report
- Analyze spending over different time periods
- Compare monthly spending
- View trends and patterns

#### Monthly Bar Chart
- Visual representation of monthly spending
- Easy-to-read bar chart format
- Compare spending across months

## Tips and Best Practices

### Organizing Expenses

1. **Use Consistent Categories**: Stick to the predefined categories for better reporting
2. **Regular Updates**: Add expenses daily or weekly to avoid forgetting transactions
3. **Detailed Descriptions**: Include relevant details in descriptions for better tracking
4. **Accurate Dates**: Use the actual date of the transaction, not when you're entering it

### Maximizing Reports

1. **Review Regularly**: Check your reports weekly or monthly to understand spending patterns
2. **Category Analysis**: Use category reports to identify areas where you spend the most
3. **Vendor Tracking**: Monitor vendor reports to see where your money goes
4. **Trend Analysis**: Use period reports to track spending trends over time

### Security

- Keep your password secure and don't share it
- Log out when using shared computers
- Use a strong, unique password

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- Verify your email and password are correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Try resetting your password (if feature available)
- Contact support if issues persist

### Expenses Not Showing

- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- Clear browser cache and try again

### Reports Not Loading

- Ensure you have expenses added
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if the issue persists
